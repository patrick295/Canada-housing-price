🍁 Canada Housing & Demographics Analysis

> Exploring homeownership, African immigration patterns, income distribution, and real estate trends across Canadian provinces using Python.

---

📌 Project Overview

This project uses the **Canada Housing Dataset** to uncover insights about property ownership, immigration trends, cost of living, and housing affordability across Canada. It is designed for data enthusiasts, researchers, and anyone curious about the Canadian real estate landscape.

---
 📊 What This Project Covers

| # | Analysis | Description |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | 🏠 **Property Ownership by Region** | How many people own land and houses across different parts of Canada |
| 2 | 🌍 **African Immigrant Homeownership** | Trends in property ownership among Africans who have moved to Canada |
| 3 | 📅 **Purchase Frequency Over Time** | The dates and years when houses were bought most frequently |
| 4 | 💰 **Average House Prices by Province** | A provincial breakdown of average real estate prices |
| 5 | 🛒 **Cost of Living** | Living expenses across Canadian provinces |
| 6 | ⚖️ **Cost of Living vs. Average Income** | How income stacks up against living costs, province by province |
| 7 | 📈 **Average Income by Province (1981–2023)** | Four decades of income data across all provinces |
| 8 | 📉 **House Price % Change (2015–2022)** | Percentage change in average house prices across Canadian provinces |
| 9 | 🌐 **African Foreign Nationals — Property Ownership** | Number of foreigners from Africa owning land or houses in **Nova Scotia**, **New Brunswick**, **Saskatchewan**, and **Alberta** |
